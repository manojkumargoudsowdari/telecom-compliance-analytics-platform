Known Constraints Summary

- category_share is Gold-defined but not persisted as a physical column in
  the first Gold implementation
- only the following Gold measures are persisted in this first
  implementation:
  - daily complaint_count
  - monthly complaint_count
  - monthly complaint_growth_rate
  - monthly rolling_average_complaint_count
- monthly Gold is derived only from daily Gold
- ijson is the approved dependency used to stream the large Silver
  JSON-array input
- validation_summary.json stores only the informational finding count, not
  the named informational entries
