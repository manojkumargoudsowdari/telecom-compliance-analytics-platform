## Purpose

## Business Context

Telecom providers receive consumer complaints through the FCC complaint process. Compliance and operations leaders need structured reporting to identify rising complaint trends, high-risk issue categories, carrier-specific patterns, and geographic concentrations that may indicate regulatory or operational risk.

## Problem Statement

Analyze FCC telecom complaint data to identify complaint trends, high-risk issue categories, geographic patterns, and carrier complaint distributions in order to support compliance monitoring and operational improvement decisions.
