## Candidate Analytical Questions

- Are telecom complaints increasing over time?
- Which carriers receive the most complaints?
- Which issue categories dominate total complaints?
- Which states produce the highest complaint volumes?
- Which carriers respond most consistently within SLA?

## Non-Goals for Phase 2

- Building the production ingestion pipeline
- Creating bronze/silver/gold transformation logic
- Publishing a final Power BI dashboard

## Assumptions

- Phase 2 requirements are limited to descriptive and diagnostic analytics.
- Requirements will be refined after initial source profiling.
